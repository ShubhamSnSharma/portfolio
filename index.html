The Builder

Flagship Project

Internships should not
be hard to find.

The Problem

Students know what they want. They don't always know where to find it. Skills, resumes, and opportunities exist as disconnected fragments.

The Approach

What if skills could lead directly to opportunities? Connecting resume credentials and real-time skill alignment into a direct recommendations course.

The Solution

InternHunt — An immersive workspace that maps a student's profile directly to relevant internships through real-time skills analysis and matching.

The Outcome

Built to bridge the gap and help students discover internships based on who they are becoming.

Core Utility

A resume tells a story.
Most never read it properly.

The Problem

Many people possess valuable skills but fail to communicate them effectively. The gap is understanding and visibility.

The Solution

Resume Analyzer — An AI-powered tool built for self-discovery. It analyzes resume content, highlights hidden strengths, and provides actionable recommendations.

The Outcome

Helping people understand their strengths before employers do.
